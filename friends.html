About us

When I am in a group of my friends, I am a talkative person; sometimes I can be a loud one in a group or just a listener. But whenever I am with my friends, I can say that is my real character. In a group, I am always the comfort one because I want my friends to put their trust in me without being forced to. Currently, we had the best moment, in which we were spending our time and sharing about our daily lives that had happened at our university. I can tell that we have a good relationship since we have been friends since 2017.

Nisa – The empathetic one of the group, Nisa is like the heart of the friendship. She’s the one everyone turns to for advice and comfort. Always with a kind word, she has a calm and gentle nature. She loves journaling her thoughts and capturing memories, making sure the group’s moments together are never forgotten. Nisa is a dreamer, with goals of becoming the best version of herself, and her resilience inspires the others.

Aleeya – The planner and problem-solver, Aleeya brings stability to the group. She’s the one who organizes their outings, makes sure everyone is okay, and keeps everything on track. With her practical and logical mindset, she can turn any challenge into a manageable situation. Aleeya has a passion for learning, always sharing interesting insights with the others, and has big ambitions for her future. Her grounded nature helps balance the group’s dynamic.

Aina – Known for her infectious energy, Aina is the life of the party. With her adventurous spirit, she’s always pushing the group out of their comfort zone to try new things. She’s spontaneous, whether it’s planning a last-minute road trip or suggesting a late-night conversation under the stars. Beneath her playful nature, Aina has a fierce loyalty, always standing up for her friends and making sure they know they’re loved.

Sarah – The creative soul, Sarah is the artistic one who sees the world through a unique lens. She’s the one who reminds her friends to look at the beauty in life’s small moments. Quietly observant but fiercely loving, Sarah’s presence brings a sense of peace to the group. She’s often the one to capture the mood of their friendships in her art, immortalizing their bond in ways that only she can.

Together, these four are more than just friends; they are like sisters, each complementing the other with their unique traits. Through ups and downs, they have built a relationship that is unbreakable, always supporting and uplifting each other in every step of their lives.
