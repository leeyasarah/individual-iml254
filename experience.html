My Experience

During my semester break from August 6 to September 29, I had the opportunity to babysit my 3-year-old niece, Naira. Since we don’t often get to spend time together, it was a special experience for both of us. Naira’s bright personality and endless curiosity kept me entertained every day. I loved watching her face light up when we played her favorite games or when she enjoyed her favorite snack like kokokrunch and chocolate biscuit. Whether we were building lego, drawing together, or watching her favorite cartoons, it was a rewarding and joyful way to spend my time off. This time together strengthened our bond, and I cherished every moment of it.

Naira's love for singing and dancing likely brought even more energy to your time together.
