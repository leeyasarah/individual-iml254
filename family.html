About My Family:

I am the fourth daughter of five children in my family. I have two older sisters, one older brother, and one younger sister. I can tell that it is hard for me to deal with my younger sister sometimes. But I am happy whenever I am with my family because I know even, we are bickering with each other, but still, at the end of the day, they are still my family, and I love them. Also, I have an older brother, with whom we currently have good relations. Sometimes he keeps disturbing me when we meet. But at that moment, I realized that the more we grow older, the more we can’t spend time together with our family members. This is because all of us have our own lives to handle, which I am a bit down to admit the reality. Now, when we have time together or when we are on a holiday, I always capture our pictures together so that I can keep the precious moments with them.





My Family's Eid video:

In a family video called "Blessing on Eid," me and my family members were gathered in a cozy living room. The day is bright, and everyone is dressed in their best clothes, ready to celebrate Eid. From the older to the younger siblings, they will hold their parents hands with both hands, asking for forgiveness and blessings from our parents. With a soft voice, they say, "Please forgive me for anything I’ve done wrong and bless me. Happy Eid!" My parents smile warmly, touching the child’s head gently and offering prayers for their happiness and success. There’s a sense of respect and care in the room as everyone takes their turn, and all of the siblings will get 'duit raya' from our parents. This is how my family will celebrate Eid every year. The video ends with the entire family sitting together, feeling at peace and grateful, having shared these special moments of asking for blessings and forgiveness on this important day of Eid.
